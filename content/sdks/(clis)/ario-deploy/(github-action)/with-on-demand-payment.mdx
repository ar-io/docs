---
title: "With On-Demand Payment"
description: "CLI for deploying apps to the permaweb (Arweave) and updating ArNS"
---

```yaml
- name: Deploy with ARIO on-demand
  uses: ar-io/ar-io-deploy@v1
  with:
    deploy-key: ${{ secrets.DEPLOY_KEY }}
    arns-key: ${{ secrets.ARNS_KEY }}
    arns-name: myapp
    deploy-folder: ./dist
    on-demand: ario
    max-token-amount: '2.0'
```
